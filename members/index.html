Step 3: Enable Hourly Cost Data

For real-time cost monitoring, enable hourly granularity in Cost Explorer. This lets SlashMyBill detect cost spikes within hours instead of days.

What it does: Enables hourly cost breakdown in AWS Cost Explorer

Cost: ~$0.01 per 1,000 API requests (typically <$1/month)

Time to activate: 24 hours after enabling

⏱ Open Cost Explorer Preferences
Check "Hourly and Resource Level Data" and click Save. You can also skip this and enable later.
⚡
