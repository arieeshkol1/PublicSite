How to download your AWS bill as PDF
  1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console with the account that pays the bill
  2. Navigate to Billing and Cost Management
  3. Select Bills from the left menu
  4. Choose the billing period you want to analyze (last month recommended)
  5. Click Print at the top of the page
  6. Under "Print contents", select all options for the most detailed report
  7. Click Print, then set Destination to "Save as PDF" and click Save
  8. Upload the saved PDF file here
