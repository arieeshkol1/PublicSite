Security Recommendations

Steps you can take to improve your privacy score and protect your online identity

🛡️ Use a VPN

A system-level VPN (not a browser extension) encrypts all traffic and hides your real IP. Recommended: NordVPN, Mullvad, ProtonVPN, or Windscribe.

🔒 Enable Do Not Track

Go to your browser privacy settings and enable "Send Do Not Track requests". While not all sites honor it, it signals your preference.

🧹 Install an Ad Blocker

uBlock Origin is the gold standard — it blocks ads, trackers, and fingerprinting scripts. Available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

🕵️ Use Private Browsing

Incognito/Private mode prevents cookies and history from persisting. Use it for sensitive browsing sessions.

🎨 Block Canvas Fingerprinting

Extensions like CanvasBlocker (Firefox) or Brave browser's built-in shields can randomize your canvas fingerprint to prevent tracking.

📡 Disable WebRTC Leaks

WebRTC can leak your real IP even behind a VPN. Disable it in browser settings or use extensions like WebRTC Leak Prevent.

🌐 Use HTTPS Everywhere

Always verify the padlock icon in your address bar. Most modern browsers enforce HTTPS, but be cautious on unfamiliar sites.

🍪 Manage Cookies

Regularly clear cookies or use auto-delete extensions like Cookie AutoDelete. Block third-party cookies in your browser settings.
